FAQ


Who can register?

Anyone undergoing formal education is eligible.

Is there an age restriction for a delegate?

Yes, you should be 15 years or older.

Do I need prior experience to appear?

No prior experience is necessary to register for the conference.

What committees are being simulated?

G20, UNHRC, UNICEF, AIPPM, FAO, UNGA, UNSC, WTO, Lok Sabha, UNW, UNODC alongside the Truth Commission.

Is prior experience needed for any of the committees?

No prior experience is required for any of the committees but familiarity will help.

For how many committees can one apply?

Delegates can apply to a maximum of 3 committee preferences, but they will be allocated only 1 committee.

When will the Conference take place?

The Conference will be held between 19 April - 21 April 2024.

How will the Conference be conducted?

EQUINOX WORLD MUN 2024 will be conducted offline at the IIIT Lucknow campus.

What will be the accommodation arrangements for delegates?

Students would be provided with accommodation in the IIIT Lucknow campus if required. Details will be released soon.

What is the dress code for the conference?

Formal attire is strongly recommended but not required.

How can I register?

Visit our registration section on this website and follow the simple steps to secure your spot at EQUINOX WORLD MUN 2024.

What is the fee?

Fee schedules can be found on our registration form. We have only a limited amount of seats, so register now!
