INVITATION FROM SECRETARY GENERAL

Dear Delegates, Respected Faculty Advisors, and the Secretariat,
I am pleased to announce the commencement of the second edition of the Equinox World Model United Nations with the theme “Jus Gentium: Law of Nations”
which highlights the importance of international cooperation and harmony to ensure worldwide peace and security.
The Equinox World Model United Nations will be a platform for diplomatic negotiations, cross cultural communication and engaging debates. It will be an opportunity for students to interact with delegates from all across the globe.
Guiding such a prestigious conference is an immense responsibility, yet with the invaluable wisdom passed down by the Chief Advisor, Dr Abhinesh Kaushik, and the exceptional dedication of our dynamic and ecstatic Secretariat, I am enthusiastic and optimistic for the enthralling experience “The Equinox World Model United Nations (MUN)” promises to deliver.
I cordially invite you to join us for this revolutionary edition, where diplomatic and collaborative discussions leads to the development of distinctive strategies.
I cordially anticipate convening with each of you at the upcoming Equinox World Model United Nations.


Yours Sincerely,

Samarth Sharma,
Secretary-General,
Equinox World MUN ’24.
